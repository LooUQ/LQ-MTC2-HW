## LQnode Labels

This folder contain artwork templates for creating your own labels to use on your LQnode project. Files suffixed with "-2425" are
for the Polycase HD-24F and HD-25F cases.


LooUQ has ordered "Matte Rectangle Stickers" from MakeStickers (https://www.makestickers.com) numerous times with success, highly 
recommended for a reasonable cost with good quality.
